بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِيْمِ

10th Muharram · Day of Ashura

Seven Prophets,
One Day of Mercy

Dua-e-Ashura — every distress answered on the same day

On the virtue of this dua: it is narrated from Imam Zainul Abideen (RA) that whoever recites it once on the Day of Ashura, between sunrise and sunset — or asks someone to recite it[...]

A Chain of Answered PrayersEach bead, a relief sent on Ashura

🤲

Adam ALAYHIS SALAM

یَا قَابِلَ تَوْبَۃِ آدَمَ یَوْمَ عَاشُوْرَآءَ

O Accepter of Adam's repentance, on the Day of Ashura.

🐋

Yunus ALAYHIS SALAM

یَا فَارِجَ کَرْبِ ذِی النُّوْنِ یَوْمَ عَاشُوْرَآءَ

O Reliever of the distress of the one swallowed by the whale, on the Day of Ashura.

🫂

Yaqub ALAYHIS SALAM

یَا جَامِعَ شَمْلِ یَعْقُوْبَ یَوْمَ عَاشُوْرَآءَ

O Gatherer of Yaqub's scattered family, on the Day of Ashura.

🦯

Musa & Harun ALAYHIMUS SALAM

یَا سَامِعَ دَعْوَۃِ مُوْسٰی وَهَارُوْنَ یَوْمَ عَاشُوْرَآءَ

O Hearer of the prayer of Musa and Harun, on the Day of Ashura.

🔥

Ibrahim ALAYHIS SALAM

یَا مُغِیْثَ اِبْرَاہِیْمَ مِنَ النَّارِ یَوْمَ عَاشُوْرَآءَ

O Rescuer of Ibrahim from the fire, on the Day of Ashura.

🪜

Idris ALAYHIS SALAM

یَا رَافِعَ اِدْرِیْسَ اِلَی السَّمَآءِ یَوْمَ عَاشُوْرَآءَ

O Raiser of Idris to the heavens, on the Day of Ashura.

🐪

Salih ALAYHIS SALAM

یَا مُجِیْبَ دَعْوَۃِ صَالِحٍ فِی النَّاقَۃِ یَوْمَ عَاشُوْرَآءَ

O Answerer of Salih's prayer concerning the she-camel, on the Day of Ashura.

☝️

Sayyiduna Muhammad SALLALLAHU ALAYHI WA SALLAM

O Most Merciful of this world and the next — send blessings upon our master Muhammad and his family, as upon every other Ashura mercy was sent down.

The Final Plea

یَا رَحْمٰنَ الدُّنْیَا وَالْاٰخِرَۃِ وَرَحِیْمَهُمَا
صَلِّ عَلٰی سَیِّدِنَا مُحَمَّدٍ وَّ�[...]

O Most Merciful of this world and the next, and the Most Compassionate of the two — send blessings upon our master Muhammad and upon the family of our master Muhammad, and send blessin[...]

اَللّٰهُمَّ بِعِزِّ الْحَسَنِ وَاَخِیْهِ وَاُمِّہٖ
وَاَبِیْہِ وَجَدِّہٖ وَبَنِیْہِ فَرِّج[...]

O Allah — by the honour of Hasan, his brother, their mother, their father, their grandfather, and his sons — relieve us from what we are in.

Then recited twelve times
